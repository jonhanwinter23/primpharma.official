We help the National Security Funds

In Cambodia, access to quality healthcare is essential for ensuring the well-being of the citizens. With the National Social Security Fund (NSSF) playing a pivotal role in providing social security coverage, PrimPharma, a new healthcare startup, has stepped forward as an invaluable volunteer in facilitating seamless access to NSSF-partnered hospitals. Through our innovative platform, PrimPharma has transformed into an information hub, bridging the gap between NSSF members and healthcare providers.

The National Social Security Fund of Cambodia stands as a beacon of support for citizens, offering comprehensive health coverage and social security benefits. However, navigating the network of NSSF-partnered hospitals can often be a daunting task for individuals seeking medical assistance. Recognizing this challenge, PrimPharma has leveraged its technological prowess to streamline the process, ensuring that members can easily locate and access NSSF-affiliated healthcare facilities.

At PrimPharma we empowers individuals to swiftly identify nearby NSSF-partnered hospitals, facilitating prompt medical attention when needed. By serving as a centralized information hub, we simplifies the search process, allowing members to make informed decisions regarding their healthcare options.
